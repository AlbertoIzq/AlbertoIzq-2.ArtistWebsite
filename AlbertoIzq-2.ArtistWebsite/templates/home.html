Welcome to my official website!
Here you will get to know me better as an artist but let me introduce myself first. I'm Alberto, a Spanish guy from Barcelona. I'm in love with music and everything related to it as you're gonna see. I consider myself as a jack-of-all-trades (master of none but pretty good at some of them, if I may say). What do I like to do? If i had been born in Middle Ages I would be considered as a minstrel. I play musical instruments, sing, dance and do funny stuff, mainly. In the begginnig I started playing the classical guitar but then I discovered beatbox. After that, I bought an electric guitar and started playing some metal. I loved metal but at the same time I couldn't cope without listening to electronic music and I liked to dance, so I started learning some shuffle moves. I was curious about bass guitar so I picked one up and started plucking with my fingers. And the list keeeeeeeeps on going. What's gonna be next? Who knows...
